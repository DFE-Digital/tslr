About this scheme

The teacher’s student loan reimbursement scheme rewards teachers for teaching priority subjects in eligible parts of England.

If you are eligible, you can claim back student loan payments for the {{globals.tax_year.full}} tax year.

Your loan can be repaid if it is was taken out with the Student Loan Company in England.

Repayments are classed as part of your pay but DfE will pay any related tax on your behalf.

We will ask the school you taught at to confirm what you tell us about your employment there.
