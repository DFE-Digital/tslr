Eligibility

To claim your student loan payments back you must:

  • be a qualified teacher
  • have qualified after 13 August 2013
  • have taught biology, chemistry, computer science, physics, languages (not English)
  • have taught one of these subjects for more than 50% of your hours in the last tax year
  • work in a state funded school (also known as a ‘public school’)
  • work in one of the participating local authority areas
