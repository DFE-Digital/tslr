Make a claim

Before you start

You will need:

  • your National insurance (NI) number
  • your sign in details for ‘Verify’ (the government’s secure way of proving who you are)

If you do not have a Verify account, you will need your a valid passport or photocard driving licence to sign up.

After you've claimed

We will ask your school to confirm that you:

  • worked at the school in the last year
  • taught one of the qualifying subjects for more than 50% of your time there
