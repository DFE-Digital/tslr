Subject

Do your teachers know about student loan reimbursement?

Body

Dear {name}

Teachers at your school may be eligible for the Teachers’ Student Loan Reimbursement (TSLR) scheme.

TSLR allows eligible teachers to claim a reimbursement for student loan payments for the preceding year. It aims to help schools in priority areas retain staff who are:

  • in years 1 to 5 of their career
  • spending at least 50% of their time teaching languages (excluding English), science or computer science.

Make sure your teachers know about the scheme

Please forward this email to teachers at your school who may be eligible and ask them to update their school email address on the database of qualified teachers (DQT).

This is important because we’ll use DQT to find eligible teachers and invite them to apply for the scheme.

For more information, read about the TSLR scheme on GOV.UK .
