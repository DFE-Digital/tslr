Eligibility

To apply, you must be in years 1 to 5 of your teaching career and fulfil all of the eligibility criteria below.

Qualified teacher status

You should have achieved qualified status between 2013 and 2020.

Employment

You should be employed in England, in one of the following state-funded schools:

  • secondary school maintained by a local authority (including middle-deemed secondary)
  • special school that is non-maintained or maintained by a local authority
  • secondary phase academy / free school

Teachers in a private school or sixth-form college are not eligible.

Participating local authorities

Your school should be in one of these local authority areas:

  • Barnsley
  • Blackpool
  • Bracknell Forest
  • Bradford
  • Cambridgeshire
  • Derby
  • Derbyshire
  • Doncaster
  • Halton
  • Knowsley
  • Luton
  • Middlesbrough
  • Norfolk
  • North-east Lincolnshire
  • North Yorkshire
  • Northamptonshire
  • Northumberland
  • Oldham
  • Peterborough
  • Portsmouth
  • Salford
  • Sefton
  • St Helens
  • Stoke-on-Trent
  • Suffolk

Subjects

You must have taught one of the following subjects for at least 50% of your contracted hours for the year you are claiming:

  • languages
  • physics
  • chemistry
  • biology
  • computer science
