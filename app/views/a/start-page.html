About this scheme

The teacherâ€™s student loan reimbursement scheme rewards teachers for teaching priority subjects in eligible parts of England.

If you are eligible, you can claim back loan repayments from the last year.

You can make an application in September after the end of an academic year.

Repayments are not classed as part of your pay. You will not have to pay tax or National Insurance on them.

We will ask the school you taught at to confirm what you tell us about your employment there.
