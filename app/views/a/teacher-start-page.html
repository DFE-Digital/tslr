Eligibility

You can apply if you have received an email from the Department of Education saying you are eligible.

You should be in years 1 to 5 of your teaching career and have achieved qualified status in or after 2013.

You must have taught one of the following priority subjects for at least 50% of your contracted hours for the year you are claiming:

  • languages (excluding English)
  • physics
  • chemistry
  • biology
  • computer science
