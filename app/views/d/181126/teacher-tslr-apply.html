Make a claim

Before you start

You will need your:

  • teacher reference number – you can get this from your school, the certificate you got when you qualified as a teacher or from the teacher qualifications helpdesk
  • National Insurance number
  • sign in details for ‘Verify’ (the government’s secure way of proving who you are) – if you do not have a Verify account, you will need your passport or photocard driving licence to sign up

We'll check your information

After you send your claim, we’ll ask your school to confirm:

  • that you worked there in the last tax year
  • that you taught one of the qualifying subjects for more than 50% of your time there
  • how much student loan you paid back through your teaching wages while you were there
