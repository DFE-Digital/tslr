Eligibility

To claim back student loan payments made through your wages between {{ globals.tax_year.start_date }} and {{ globals.tax_year.end_date }} (the last tax year), you must:

  • have qualified as a teacher after {{ globals.eligibility_criteria.qualified_date }}
  • still be teaching in a school in the UK
  • worked at a state-funded school (also known as a ‘public school’) in the last tax year
  • worked at a school that’s in a participating local authority area in England (areas where there are fewer teachers in these subjects at the moment) in the last tax year

In the last tax year you must also have taught either:

  • biology, chemistry, computer science, physics, languages (not English) for more than 50% of your hours at the school
  • in a special school (a school that teaches children who have special educational needs)

Participating local authority areas

The school you taught at must be in one of these local authority areas:

  • Barnsley
  • Blackpool
